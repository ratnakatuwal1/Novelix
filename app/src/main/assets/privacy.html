Novelix Privacy Policy

At Novelix, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our novel reading application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with an exceptional reading experience, we collect various types of information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, and optional profile details.
  • Reading Activity: We track your reading progress, bookmarks, library selections, and preferences to enhance your experience.
  • Device Data: We collect information about your device including model, operating system, unique identifiers, and network information.
  • Usage Analytics: We gather data about feature usage, session duration, and interaction patterns to improve our services.

Note: We implement strict data minimization principles, collecting only what is necessary to deliver and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data enables us to provide personalized and secure services:

  • Customize your reading experience with tailored recommendations
  • Develop and enhance app functionality and performance
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send service-related communications and (with consent) promotional content
  • Monitor and maintain app security and integrity

3. Information Sharing Practices

We value your trust and maintain strict confidentiality:

  • Service Partners: We engage carefully vetted third-party providers for infrastructure, analytics, and payment processing under strict contractual obligations.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.
  • Business Transitions: In mergers or acquisitions, user information may be transferred as a business asset with appropriate protections.

4. Your Privacy Rights

You maintain control over your personal information:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information through app settings
  • Adjust communication preferences in your account dashboard
  • Request data export or deletion by contacting our privacy team
  • Withdraw consent for specific processing activities

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy regulations.

5. Data Security Measures

We implement robust security protocols including:

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Comprehensive employee training on data protection

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use essential and functional cookies to:

  • Maintain user sessions and preferences
  • Analyze service performance and usage patterns
  • Support security features and fraud prevention

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though this may impact functionality.

7. Children's Privacy Protection

Our services are designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will promptly delete the information and terminate the account.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed globally. We ensure adequate safeguards through:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Data processing agreements with international partners
  • Adherence to international privacy frameworks

9. Policy Updates

We may periodically update this policy to reflect:

  • Changes in our services or data practices
  • Evolving legal and regulatory requirements
  • Feedback from our user community

Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or email.
